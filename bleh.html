About Me

I am a first-year Mechanical Engineering student at BMS College of Engineering — one of Karnataka's most respected engineering institutions. From the beginning, what drew me to mechanical engineering was not just machines, but the design decisions behind them.

I approach engineering with what I call rational outrage — a disciplined analytical mind that questions why things are designed the way they are, then works systematically to imagine them better. I do not accept convention as justification.

I am building a foundation across CAD tools, computational languages, and manufacturing fundamentals — not to learn them passively, but to develop the depth needed to lead design decisions at an advanced level. I am in this for the long game.

I communicate clearly, collaborate effectively, and take responsibility seriously. I understand that engineering is a team effort and that the quality of your work reflects your character.

Hands-On Exposure

Industrial Visit 7 Days · 2024
Kinetic Engineering Ltd. — Electric Vehicle Manufacturing Division

Kinetic E-Scooter Production Facility

Completed a structured 7-day industrial exposure programme at Kinetic Engineering's electric scooter manufacturing plant — one of India's established EV manufacturing units. The visit provided end-to-end visibility into a live production environment, covering component fabrication workflows, assembly line sequencing, quality assurance protocols, and electric powertrain integration procedures.

Key observations included how tolerance specifications from CAD drawings are enforced on the shop floor, how production constraints shape design decisions upstream, and how cross-departmental coordination sustains manufacturing efficiency. This experience provided early, direct context for how academic engineering principles are applied — and constrained — in real-world production settings.
